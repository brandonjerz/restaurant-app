Our Story

Dough Boy’s is a locally owned family business founded in 1989 in the surf-town of Virginia Beach, VA, a sunny vacation-land that plays host to millions of visitors annually. Back then the pizza concept was called Dough Boy’s Pasta and Grill, and there was one Dough Boy’s, located at 2410 Atlantic Avenue in the heart of the resort.
